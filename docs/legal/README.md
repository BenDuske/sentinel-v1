# Legal & Policy Documents

These documents govern use of **Sentinel v1** (local AI risk & incident intelligence). They are
part of the open-source release.

> ⚠️ **DRAFT — pending attorney review.** Every document in this folder is a good-faith
> template, **not legal advice**, and has **not** been reviewed by a licensed attorney. If you
> deploy this Software commercially, have your own counsel review and adapt these before you
> rely on them.

| Document | What it covers |
|----------|----------------|
| [`ACCEPTABLE_USE_POLICY.md`](ACCEPTABLE_USE_POLICY.md) | What you may and may not do — lawful-use (U.S. federal + your state/local law), prohibited content (no nudity/sexual content; zero tolerance for sexualizing minors; no harm-enabling content), and the fact that documenting real incidents is the legitimate purpose. High-stakes-use limits. |
| [`PRIVACY_POLICY.md`](PRIVACY_POLICY.md) | Local-first data handling; incident data lives in a local SQLite store and evidence in a local uploads dir; the author collects nothing; the default LLM is local Ollama. |
| [`TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md`](TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md) | License conditions, no-reliance on AI output, no warranty, liability limit, indemnification, governing law (Texas). |
| [`DISCLAIMER.md`](DISCLAIMER.md) | AI-output and warranty disclaimer — output is decision-support not authoritative; may be wrong; human-in-the-loop. |
