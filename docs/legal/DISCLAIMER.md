## AI-generated output
This Software uses large language models to draft incident **severity scores, summaries, and
recommended actions**. Its output is **probabilistic and can be wrong** — inaccurate,
incomplete, outdated, biased, or entirely fabricated ("hallucinated"), even when it sounds
confident and cites specifics.

- The output is **assistive, not authoritative.** It is **risk decision-support, not an
  underwriting, claims, insurance, or safety determination.**
- It is **not** professional advice — not legal, financial, insurance, medical, engineering, or
  safety advice.
- **Always verify** anything important against primary sources and qualified human experts
  before acting on it.
- A **human must remain in the loop** for any consequential decision. The Software is built
  around this (Human-Centered AI): every AI output is shown with its rationale and is editable
  before export. Do not wire the Software's raw output into automated high-stakes actions
  without human review.

## Severity scoring & accuracy
Severity is produced by a deterministic rule layer reconciled with the LLM, with a rationale
showing both. This makes the score **more defensible, not infallible.** Treat it as a starting
point for a human reviewer, not a final verdict.
